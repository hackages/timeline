Key Points

Pair programming

Pair programming is a core part of our methodology. The first day, you'll build an algorythm that will randomly assign you a partner for a day or just one exercise.

Pair programming is composed of a driver and an observer. The two programmers change roles frequently. There a lot advantages to that approach in term of learning, team-building and communication

Mentorship program

Everyday, we invite industry experts to come and share with you their experience with these techonologies. They will sometimes lead a lecture on E2E testing, Unit testing, Why you should use TypeScript in your project and many more topics...

Moreover, we welcome former bootcampers to help you with your exercises and share with you their experience using all the tools they learn during their session.

Live coding session

We start every session with a live coding session on the topic we're about to cover. We quickstart the session by brainstorming possible solutions as a team then you pair with another attendee to complete the exercise.

At the end of the exercise, we choose one team to come up and showcase how they complete their task. This approach led us to better results in teaching key concepts with ease.
